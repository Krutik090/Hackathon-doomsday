Frequently Asked

Got
questions?

Who can join Hackathon 2026 Doomsday Edition?
+

Open to undergraduate and postgraduate students from any recognized university or college across India. Early-career professionals (up to 2 years of experience) are also welcome. Teams of 2–4 members are recommended, though solo warriors are accepted.

Is there a registration fee?
+

There is a nominal registration fee to confirm your participation. Early-bird registrations (before May 1, 2026) receive a significant discount. The fee covers all meals, accommodation options, swag, and event access for the full 48 hours.

Can I participate in multiple tracks?
+

Each team must submit to one primary track. However, projects that creatively span multiple domains (e.g., AI + Cybersecurity) are absolutely encouraged and may be eligible for cross-track special prizes as determined by judges.

Will there be mentorship and resources available?
+

Yes! We will have 25+ mentors available throughout the event including Tribastion security researchers, CHARUSAT faculty, and industry professionals. Cloud credits, API access, and development resources will be provided to all registered teams upon confirmation.

What should I bring to the hackathon?
+

Bring your laptop, charger, valid college ID or government ID, and your most ambitious ideas. Accommodation will be arranged for outstation participants. All hardware, common tools, and food will be provided by the organizers. Projects must be started from scratch at the event.

How will projects be judged?
+

Projects are evaluated on four criteria: Technical Complexity (25%), Innovation & Originality (25%), Real-World Impact (25%), and Presentation & Demo Quality (25%). All finalist teams will present to a panel of industry judges on the final day.

When will registrations open?
+

Registrations open in April 2026. We strongly encourage signing up early as participation is capped to ensure quality mentorship and resources. Drop your email via the contact section to get notified the moment registrations go live.
